FAQ

Why pre-order vs walking in?

Pre-order gets you the latest drops at supplier-direct pricing — typically 8-25% off retail. Plus you get first dibs on limited drops (GX1000 SU26, Theories prebook) before they hit the floor.

What if you sell out before my order ships?

You're locked in once you submit before Sunday midnight. We've already pre-ordered against the projected demand — your specific items are reserved.

Can I pick up at the shop?

Yes — pickup is the default. 1 Highland Industrial Park, Peekskill, NY 10566. Local Westchester delivery is free. Shipping anywhere else is at-cost (no markup).

How do I pay? (50% deposit · 50% on pickup or +shipping)

Two payments:

  • 50% deposit due now — locks in pricing + reserves your gear. Within hours of submitting we text a secure card link (Helcim). Venmo + Zelle work too.
  • Remaining 50% when your gear is ready — at pickup that's the flat balance. If you chose shipping, the second payment is 50% balance + actual shipping cost.

Cancel anytime before Sunday 6pm ET for a full deposit refund. After that the deposit is non-refundable since we've placed the bulk supplier order.

What if my order doesn't arrive in 2 weeks?

Most orders ship within 1 week — 2 is a generous outer bound. We text the moment your stuff lands. If it's been 2+ weeks with no update, your full deposit is refundable.

Sizes / colors not listed — can I request?

Absolutely. Type the size/color you want when you submit. We'll text you back with availability + final pricing.
