DecisionLayer · AI arbitration in the contract layer

decisionlayer.ai · piloting with 2nd Nature Park waivers

We integrate AI arbitration into clients' contracts as part of the build. DecisionLayer is an online AI dispute-resolution platform built by Liberty McAteer — disputes resolved in ~45-90 days for ~$500, versus the $100k+/18-month standard of traditional litigation. We're an early-adopter integration partner: every Production Sprint can ship with the DecisionLayer arbitration clause already in your contract templates. You don't just leave with software — you leave with a contract stack that handles disputes the new way.

  • Why it matters: Most software gets shipped with a generic "we'll see you in court" clause from a 2002 template. DecisionLayer turns that clause into a working AI arbitration system on day one.
  • Pre-shipped with skateOS waivers: the same waiver flow we built for 2nd Nature Park is being upgraded to ship with the AI arbitration clause baked in.
  • Battle-tested already: Liberty ran a simulated injury claim (plaintiff alleging $35k in damages from a ramp defect) against our waiver + his clause. System ruled for the operator. We saw the receipts.
  • Standard inclusion on Production Sprint: if your build needs a customer-facing contract or waiver, we drop the clause in. No extra charge.
  • Optional opt-in for Spike + Fix-it Sprint — we'll wire it up if you want it.
