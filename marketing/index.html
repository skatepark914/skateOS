Built for park ops

Run the whole park from one screen.

Front desk, shop, lessons, parties, pre-orders, mobile shop, payroll, marketing, customer self-serve. No duct tape.

🛹

Live Check-In

QR member cards, voice search, real-time "in the park now" board, punch-card auto-decrement, waiver-first flow, family group detection, capacity warnings. Built for the iPad at the front desk.

💳

Point of Sale + Helcim

Tap-to-pay via HelcimPay, hosted invoice links auto-emailed, automatic receipts, split payments, gift cards, comp/void/discount approval gates. Lower in-person fees than Square.

🎓

Lessons & camps

Calendar with private/group/camp/event/birthday types, auto 24-hr SMS+email reminders, post-lesson follow-up with review request, full-screen camp roster check-in for counselors, conflict detection, skill tracker, recurrence builder.

🪪

Memberships & punch cards

Day pass / monthly / annual / punch card / industry comp. Pause-and-resume with auto-resume cron, renewal reminder cron, bulk-extend for closures, low-punch detection. Print laminate member cards with QR.

⭐

Loyalty + tiers + perks

Auto-earn on sales + check-ins + lessons attended. Bronze/Silver/Gold tiers with owner-tunable thresholds + perks. Gift points between members. Refund-aware (auto-reverses points on refunded sales). Atomic redeem.

📋

Pre-order workflow

White-label catalog at preorder.<yoursite>.com. Customer pre-orders with 50/50 deposit-on-submit + balance-on-pickup. Weekly Sunday-cutoff cron emails the supplier-rollup as a CSV. Status page, Helcim auto-payment-link, deposit-paid auto-flip via webhook.

🏆

Affiliate / team riders

Enroll customers as team riders with their own promo code. Auto-commission on every code-attributed sale. Public team page + per-rider profile + printable shop-wall poster with QR code. Power-user candidate detection identifies who'd be a good fit.

📧

Marketing automation

Pre-stocked campaign templates (welcome, birthday, low-punch, lapsed, Gold-tier appreciation, pre-order cutoff). Tag + tier + segment-based targeting. A/B test split with deterministic 50/50 hashing. CAN-SPAM opt-out compliant. Daily/weekly digest emails to owner.

🚌

Mobile bus shop

Take a curated catalog to other parks. Inventory persists on the bus between runs. Sales auto-tag the active run. End-of-run reconcile flow with cash variance + per-product physical count. Bus inventory + base inventory tracked separately.

🚨

Incidents + safety

Skater-shaped form: park area, helmet status, guardian contact, severity ladder. Auto-emails + texts the owner on ER/911. Searchable history for insurance. Reports card surfaces incident hotspots by park area.

🛡️

Equipment / loaners

Per-asset loaner tracking with signature pad + condition photo capture (before/after rental). Inspection cycle audit-stamps with overdue alert. Fleet status report card. Insurance-ready exports.

🕐

Staff timesheets + chat

Atomic clock-in/out via RPC, owner-approved weekly payroll, server-side audit trail. Mid-shift role switch (front desk → instructor mid-day). End-of-shift handoff modal with cash count. Internal team chat with assignable reminders.

👤

Customer self-serve portal

Magic-link sign-in, no passwords. Customers see their loyalty + tier + visits + active passes + upcoming lessons + recent purchases + pre-orders + achievement badges. Edit profile. Cancel lesson. Print own member card. CAN-SPAM unsubscribe.

📊

30+ Reports cards

Daily close-out with cash drawer count + Helcim deposit reconcile. Partner P&L splits, in-park heatmap, weekend mix, lesson cancellation reasons, refund rate by product, customer LTV distribution, MRR/churn trend, cash variance trend, equipment fleet status, instructor reliability + utilization. CSV exports throughout.

🎁

Gift cards

Sell at POS with personalized message + recipient email. Public balance-check page (/gift-card.html?code=…). Bulk B2B sale flow for corporate / sponsor packages. Append-only ledger for forensic audit trail. Daily liability snapshot in owner digest.

📝

Forms / booking landing

Public-renderer form builder. Lesson intake / birthday-party inquiry / general contact / customer feedback templates. Each form gets a shareable URL. One-click "book lesson" / "add as customer" smart conversion from submissions.
