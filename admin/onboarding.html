STEP 6 · Integrations

The things you'll wire later

These need accounts you sign up for and API keys you paste in. Skip for now and revisit in Settings → Integrations any time.

  • 💳 Helcim — card processing
    Sign up at helcim.com, get an API token, paste into Settings → Integrations. Interchange-plus pricing typically beats Square by ~$6k/year at moderate volume.
  • 📧 Resend — email (receipts, reminders, marketing)
    Verify your sending domain at resend.com and paste the API key. Without this, automated emails won't fire.
  • 📱 Twilio — SMS (lesson reminders, marketing)
    Sign up at twilio.com, buy a number, paste your SID + token. Optional — skip if you only want email.
  • 🛡️ Smartwaiver — digital liability waivers
    Optional. Without it, you can still mark paper waivers in skateOS. Sign up at smartwaiver.com if you want skaters to sign on their phone before they arrive.

Setup complete

Your business info, hours, tax, tips, and starter products are saved. Jump into the admin to start running your park.

  • Add your team
    Staff page → invite cashiers, instructors, monitors. Assign roles so they can clock in.
  • Print member cards
    Tools → Print Member Cards. Hand to skaters at signup → scan at the front desk.
  • Make social graphics
    Tools → Social Graphics. Generate brand-consistent Facebook, IG, TikTok profile pics + cover art in one click.
  • Test a sale
    POS page → ring up a $0.01 test sale to make sure receipts, loyalty, and inventory all fire.
