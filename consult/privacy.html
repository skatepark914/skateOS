Hudson Valley AI / Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-05-10 · last reviewed 2026-05-10

Hudson Valley AI is a principal-led software + security consultancy based in the Hudson Valley region of New York. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what you can do about it. Plain English. No tracking-cookie roundabout.

1. Who this applies to

This covers visitors to consult.skateos.com, skateos.com/consulting, and any other Hudson Valley AI marketing surface we operate. It does not cover client-system data we process when delivering an engagement — that's governed by the project's specific data-handling terms in the engagement letter.

2. What we collect

Only what's needed to respond to inquiries and operate the site:

  • Intake form data — name, email, phone, company, project details, budget, and any free-text you submit.
  • Email correspondence — when you reply to our emails, we keep the thread.
  • Web analytics — Cloudflare provides aggregated, privacy-friendly analytics (page views, referrers, country-level geolocation). No tracking cookies, no third-party trackers, no fingerprinting.
  • Server logs — IP addresses and request headers in our hosting provider's logs, retained for 7 days for security / abuse purposes.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking. There are no marketing cookies on this site.

3. Why we collect it

  • Respond to your inquiry and prepare a written proposal.
  • Get you started as a customer if we're a fit.
  • Send transactional emails about engagements you've started (proposals, invoices, deliverables).
  • Detect and prevent spam / abuse on public forms.

4. Who we share it with

Two categories, both narrow:

  • Sub-processors we use to operate the site: Resend (email delivery), Cloudflare (hosting + DNS + analytics). Both have signed industry-standard data-processing terms.
  • Legal requirements — if compelled by lawful court order or subpoena. We'll notify you before complying where legally permitted.

We never sell your data. We don't share intake-form contents with anyone outside Doug + Jon and the sub-processors above.

5. How long we keep it

  • Intake form data — kept for 24 months from submission (to support follow-up), then deleted.
  • Active customer correspondence — kept for the duration of the engagement plus 7 years (tax records).
  • Server logs — 7 days.
  • Cloudflare analytics — anonymized, kept per Cloudflare's defaults.

6. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy laws. Regardless of jurisdiction, you can always:

  • Request a copy of any data we hold about you. Email doug@2ntr.com; we'll respond within 30 days.
  • Request deletion of your data. Same email; we'll confirm in writing within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g. tax records).
  • Correct inaccurate information we hold about you.
  • Withdraw consent for any non-essential emails.

7. Security

Our security practices are documented at consult.skateos.com/security. Short version: encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+), encrypted at rest (AES-256 via cloud provider), hardware MFA on privileged access.

8. Children

Our services are intended for businesses, not children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16.

9. Changes

If we change this policy materially we'll notify any active customers in writing. The version date at the top reflects the most recent change.
