FAQ

What operators ask first

How long does setup take?

Typically 1-2 weeks. We migrate your existing customer + membership data, configure your branding, wire up Smartwaiver + payments + Brivo if you have it, train your front desk on the iPad app. Most parks are taking real transactions within a week.

What if we already have Brivo / Smartwaiver / Helcim accounts?

We use what you have. Hand us your API credentials (BYO model) and we hook in. No replacing hardware, no contract changes with those vendors.

Do you replace Square POS hardware?

No — skateOS runs on any iPad. If you have Square card readers, keep them; we recommend Helcim for new installs (better in-person rates, ~$6k/year savings at typical skatepark volume). Bring your existing receipt printer too.

What about white-label — can the customer-facing pages look like our brand?

Yes. Your member portal, public landing, signup, booking, gift card balance — all branded for your park. Per-park theme color, logo, copy. Same software stack, your colors.

Who owns the data?

You do. Hosted in your own Supabase project (or our managed one — your call). Export everything to CSV anytime. Cancel anytime + take your data with you.

How does support work?

Email + shared Slack channel for Pro. SLA + phone for Enterprise. We ship features multiple times a week — your requests + ours get prioritized as they land.

Are you guys actually skaters?

Yes. Doug + Jon co-own 2nd Nature Park in Peekskill, NY. Both lifelong skaters. The software exists because we needed it ourselves.

Want to see it running a real park?

45-minute video call. We walk you through 2nd Nature's live install — POS, memberships, door access, reports. Show us your park, we'll show you what skateOS would look like running it.
