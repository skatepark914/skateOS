Built for park ops

Run the whole park from one screen.

Front desk, shop, lessons, parties, mobile shop, payroll. No duct tape.

🛹

Live Check-In

QR member cards, real-time "in the park now" board, punch-card auto-decrement, waiver-first flow. Built for the iPad at the front desk.

💳

Point of Sale + Helcim

Tap-to-pay via HelcimPay, hosted invoice links to text/email customers, automatic receipts via Resend. Lower in-person fees than Square.

🎓

Lessons & camps

Calendar with private/group/camp/event/birthday types, auto 24-hr SMS+email reminders, per-instructor filters, capacity tracking.

🪪

Memberships & punch cards

Day pass / monthly / annual / punch card / industry comp. Print laminate member cards with QR. Auto-flag expiring memberships.

⭐

Loyalty points

Auto-earn on sales + check-ins, owner-tunable rates, atomic redeem. Built in — no Square Loyalty add-on subscription.

🚌

Mobile bus shop

Take a curated catalog to other parks. Inventory persists on the bus between runs. Sales auto-tag; reconciles taken vs sold vs returned with variance flagging.

🚨

Incidents

Skater-shaped form: park area, helmet status, guardian contact, severity ladder. Auto-emails + texts the owner on ER/911. Searchable history for insurance.

🕐

Staff timesheets + chat

Atomic clock-in/out via RPC, owner-approved weekly payroll, server-side audit trail. Internal team chat with assignable reminders.

📊

Reports + reconcile

Daily close-out with cash drawer count + Helcim deposit reconcile. Partner P&L splits, in-park heatmap, base vs mobile bus revenue split.
