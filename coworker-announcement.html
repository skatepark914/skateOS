🛹 Team Update

We're building our own system.

Replacing Square with something built for a skate park — by us, for us.

Same job for you day-to-day. Better tools. Way less money going to processing fees. And features Square literally can't do.

Why we're doing this

  1. Square is expensive. We're paying around $775/month in card-processing fees — roughly $9,300/year going to Square.
  2. We don't own our data. Our customers, sales history, and memberships all live inside Square's system. If their prices jump, we're stuck.
  3. Square doesn't do what a skate park needs. No real punch cards. No lesson calendar. No birthday-party bookings. No waiver integration. We've been working around it for years.

What this means for you

Mostly nothing is changing about your shift. You'll still check people in, ring up day passes and retail, run the cash drawer, and process card payments.

What will change

  • Your own login — email + password per person, instead of the shared iPad login. If someone leaves, we deactivate their account, not change the password for everyone.
  • Face ID sign-in once we're on the new app — no typing passwords.
  • Faster check-ins — search a skater, tap "Check In," done. Pulls waiver status from Smartwaiver automatically.
  • Real member QR cards — scannable cards for one-tap check-in.
  • Mobile payments — Tap to Pay on iPhone means instructors can take payment for a lesson right there at the bowl, no card reader.

The money side — monthly ops

Here's what we're paying Square today vs. what the new system will cost us:

What we pay for Square (now) New system
Card processing fees ~$775/mo ~$525/mo
Software / features $0–165/mo $25/mo
Backups, email, SMS (bundled) ~$35/mo
Cyber insurance $0 ~$50/mo
Total per month ~$775–940 ~$635
Total per year ~$9,300–11,300 ~$7,600
Annual savings ~$1,700–3,700 per year — forever

Savings grow as we grow. Every extra $1,000 in revenue through Square costs us another $26 in fees. Through the new system: $8.80.

Net hardware cost after reselling: ~$200–300 all-in. That's one week of processing-fee savings.
