The actual engagement terms for a project are in a written engagement letter we send before any work starts — that signed letter controls if there's any conflict with this page.

Part A — website terms of use

By visiting consult.skateos.com or any Hudson Valley AI marketing surface, you agree to:

  • Use the site for lawful purposes only.
  • Not attempt to bypass security controls (rate limits, anti-spam, etc.).
  • Submit truthful information through our intake form.
  • Respect our copyright in the site's content. You may quote brief excerpts with attribution; substantial reuse needs written permission.

Part B — how a paid engagement works

1. The engagement letter

Before any work starts we send a written engagement letter — typically one page — describing scope, deliverables, schedule, total fee, and any exclusions. Both sides sign it. That document is the contract for the engagement. We keep the paperwork concrete and short by design — long-form MSA templates are appropriate at a different deal size.

2. Fees & payment

  • Fixed-scope packages (Spike, Production Sprint, Security Look-Over, Fix-it Sprint) — 50% deposit on signing, 50% on delivery.
  • Late payment — 1.5% / month interest after 30 days past due. Engagement may pause until current.
  • Spike refund — the Spike package ($5k, 1 week) is fully refundable if you walk before day 3. Other packages pro-rate by completed milestone.

3. Intellectual property

  • You own the deliverables. Full IP transfer to you on final payment. We retain no rights to your code, designs, or content.
  • We retain rights to general-purpose tooling we built before or use across projects (libraries, build scripts, internal tooling). Those are licensed to you for your engagement but not exclusively.
  • Pre-existing IP — anything we owned before the engagement remains ours. Anything you owned before remains yours.

4. Confidentiality

If you want an NDA, we'll sign one — mutual, standard form. Even without one, we don't disclose your code, designs, or commercial details to third parties, and we don't name you publicly without your written OK.

5. Warranties

  • We warrant that the work is original, performed competently, and free from known defects at delivery.
  • We don't warrant uninterrupted operation, fitness for any purpose beyond what's in the engagement letter, or that bugs will never occur.
  • Bug fixes within the 30-day post-delivery support window are included free.

6. Liability

  • Cap on liability — limited to fees paid in the 12 months preceding the claim, except for cases of gross negligence, willful misconduct, or breach of confidentiality.
  • No consequential damages — neither side is liable for indirect, special, consequential, or punitive damages.
  • If your engagement has specific insurance / indemnification requirements, raise them during scoping and we'll address them in the engagement letter.

7. Termination

  • Either side may terminate for material breach with 30 days' written notice and a cure period.
  • You may terminate for convenience by paying for completed work to date plus a 25% kill fee on remaining scope (Spike is exempt — see above).
  • We may terminate for non-payment after 30 days past due with 7 days' cure notice.
  • On termination, we deliver work-in-progress, return your data, and revoke our access. See our security practices.

8. No subcontracting

We don't subcontract or outsource your work without your written consent. The principals named on this site are the people who do the work on your project.

9. Dispute resolution

  • Talk first. 30 days of direct conversation before any formal action.
  • If we built DecisionLayer into your contracts — same applies between us. Disputes route through DecisionLayer's AI arbitration ($500, 45-90 days).
  • Otherwise — mediation, then arbitration (JAMS or AAA), in Westchester County, New York.
  • Governing law — State of New York.

10. Changes to these terms

This page applies to the website itself. The engagement letter signed for a specific project controls that project's terms regardless of changes here. We may update this page; updates apply to new visitors and new engagements only.
