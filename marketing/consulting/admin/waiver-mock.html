This is what you'd walk into the first Rock Club meeting with — a printed 1-page mock showing what their liability waiver would look like after a Production Sprint. Paragraph 6 is the DecisionLayer arbitration clause (highlighted blue). Everything else is a representative composite of standard climbing-gym waiver language; the point isn't to deliver finished legal text — that's their attorney's job — it's to show that we know exactly where the new piece goes and what it does.

  • Use the printout, not the screen. Hand it to them across the table. People react differently to paper.
  • Don't lecture about paragraph 6. Let them notice it. If they don't, point and say "this is the part that turns a $100k lawsuit into a $500 AI arbitration."

1. Activity acknowledged

1.1 The Participant is voluntarily participating in indoor rock climbing — including top-rope, lead, and bouldering — at The Rock Club's facility in New Rochelle, NY ("the Facility"). Participant acknowledges that climbing involves inherent physical risk, exertion, and the use of safety equipment and techniques that require attentiveness and skill.

2. Assumption of risk

2.1 Participant understands that climbing, belaying, falling, swinging, and equipment use carry risks including but not limited to bruises, sprains, fractures, head and neck injury, abrasions from rope or wall surfaces, and in rare cases serious injury or death. Participant assumes all such risks, known and unknown, and accepts personal responsibility for the consequences of participation.

3. Release of liability

3.1 In consideration for being permitted to participate, Participant (and Participant's heirs, executors, and assigns) releases and discharges The Rock Club, Pine Brook Fitness, their owners, officers, employees, instructors, and agents from any and all claims, demands, actions, or causes of action arising out of or related to Participant's use of the Facility, including those caused in whole or in part by the negligence of the Released Parties, to the fullest extent permitted by law.

4. Indemnification

4.1 Participant agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Released Parties from any loss, liability, damage, or cost (including reasonable attorneys' fees) that the Released Parties may incur due to Participant's participation, except to the extent caused by the gross negligence or willful misconduct of a Released Party.

5. Photographic & media release

5.1 Participant consents to the use of photographs and video taken at the Facility, in which Participant may appear, for The Rock Club's promotional, marketing, and social-media purposes. Participant waives any right of inspection, review, or compensation for such use. Participant may withdraw this consent in writing at any time as to future use.

6. Dispute resolution — AI arbitration via DecisionLayerNEW

The replacement for "we'll see you in court"

6.1 Any dispute, claim, or controversy arising out of or relating to this Release, the Participant's use of the Facility, or any injury or loss alleged to have occurred at the Facility, shall be resolved exclusively through binding arbitration administered by DecisionLayer.ai, in accordance with the DecisionLayer Arbitration Rules then in effect (decisionlayer.ai/arbitrationclause).

6.2 The arbitration will be conducted online, with disputes typically resolved in 45-90 days at a cost of approximately $500 per matter, instead of traditional court litigation. The DecisionLayer process applies established legal principles to a complete record of evidence submitted by both parties; the system operates independently of both parties and is not "coached" by either side.

6.3 By signing this Release, Participant waives the right to a jury trial and to participate in any class, collective, or representative action against the Released Parties. This provision does not preclude either party from seeking emergency injunctive relief in a court of competent jurisdiction. This Section 6 survives termination of Participant's membership or participation.

7. Governing law & severability

7.1 This Release is governed by the laws of the State of New York, without regard to its conflict-of-laws principles. If any portion of this Release is held to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in full force and effect.

8. Acknowledgment

8.1 Participant has read this Release in its entirety, understands its terms (including the arbitration provision in Section 6), and signs voluntarily. If signing on behalf of a minor, the parent or legal guardian represents that they have full authority to bind the minor to the terms herein.
