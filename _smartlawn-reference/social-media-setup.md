## Quick Setup Priority Order

If you cannot set up all 8 platforms at once, prioritize in this order:

1. **Google Business Profile** -- Immediate local SEO impact, shows up in Google Maps and local search results. This is the single most important platform.
2. **Facebook Business Page** -- Largest general audience, enables Facebook/Instagram ads, community engagement.
3. **Instagram Business** -- Visual platform ideal for showcasing robotic mowers in action, connects to Facebook.
4. **Nextdoor Business** -- Hyperlocal reach to verified homeowners in your service area.
5. **YouTube Channel** -- Long-form content has long shelf life, great for search (YouTube is the second-largest search engine).
6. **TikTok Business** -- Fastest-growing platform, ideal for short demo videos that can go viral.
7. **X (Twitter)** -- Good for industry news, brand presence, and quick customer engagement.
8. **LinkedIn Company Page** -- Professional credibility, B2B networking, hiring.
