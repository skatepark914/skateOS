## 1. Facebook Business Page

### Creation URL
https://www.facebook.com/pages/create

### Step-by-Step Setup

1. Log in with a personal Facebook account (this becomes the Page admin — use the owner account, not staff).
2. **Page Name:** `2nd Nature Park`
3. **Category:** type `Skateboard Park` and select. Add up to 3:
   - Skateboard Park
   - Sports & Recreation
   - Sporting Goods Store
4. **Bio:** paste the Facebook bio (140 chars) from above.
5. Click **Create Page**.

### Why bother with LinkedIn for a skatepark?

It's not for customer acquisition. It IS for:
- School / district / camp partnerships (Westchester PE coordinators are on LinkedIn)
- Corporate party / team-building leads
- Hiring instructors and coaches
- Local business / chamber-of-commerce networking
- Press / journalist outreach (Hudson Valley Magazine, lohud, Patch)

Post once a week minimum: hiring announcements, partnership wins, owner thought-leadership, community-impact stats.

Nextdoor is hyper-local. Parents asking "where can my kid skate around here?" land there first. A solid Nextdoor presence converts at 2–3× of FB ads for $0.

### First Actions

- Verify business (address postcard)
- Post a friendly intro in Peekskill, Cortlandt, Yorktown, Ossining, Mount Kisco, Briarcliff feeds — "we're the indoor skatepark on Highland Ave, here's what we offer, AMA"
- Respond to every "kids skate park near me?" thread within 24 hours

## Settings → Social handles wiring

Once accounts are live, paste handles into the admin at **Settings → Business → Social handles**:

| Field | Format | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Instagram | `@handle` or full URL | `2ndnaturepark` |
| Facebook | `@handle` or page URL | `2ndNaturePark` |
| TikTok | `@handle` | `2ndnaturepark` |
| YouTube | `@handle` or channel URL | `2ndnaturepark` |
| X (Twitter) | `@handle` | `2ndnaturepark` |
| LinkedIn | `company/<slug>` or full URL | `company/2nd-nature-park` |

The admin persists these to `settings.socials` and uses them on:
- Receipt footers (in-app + emailed + thermal)
- Marketing email footers
- The public Team Riders page (when migration 045 applied)
- The customer self-serve portal home
