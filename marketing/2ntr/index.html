FAQ

Stuff people ask

How does 24/7 access actually work?

We use Brivo cloud-controlled door locks. When you sign up as a monthly or annual member, we provision a credential in Brivo. They email you a Mobile Pass invite. You install the Brivo Mobile app, accept the pass, and from that point on your phone unlocks the park door via Bluetooth — just walk up and tap. No keys, no codes.

What if I lose my phone or it dies?

The park door has an intercom — press it and you ring the front desk during staffed hours. Outside staffed hours, your phone is the only way in, so keep it charged. If you genuinely lose your phone, message us and we'll reset your credential before close of business that day.

Are kids allowed? What's the deal with minors?

Yes — we host kids from about 6+ regularly. Anyone under 18 needs a parent or guardian on file with their own contact info. The waiver has to be signed by the guardian, not the skater. Parents are welcome on the deck — there's seating + WiFi.

Do you have rental gear / loaners?

Helmets are free loaners — we have a bin by the front desk in adult + kid sizes. Boards, pads, and shoes are rental ($5/session) but we recommend bringing your own once you've decided to stick with it.

Can I bring a friend who's not a member?

Yes — guests pay the day pass rate ($25 adult / $18 under 12). Monthly + annual members get a discounted guest rate on Saturdays. If you're throwing a birthday party, that's a separate package — see info@2ntr.com.

What if I want to pause my membership?

Self-serve pause via your member portal — pick a return date. While paused you don't get charged + your 24/7 access is suspended. We auto-resume on your chosen date.

Do you offer lessons?

Yes — private lessons, group lessons, beginner camps, and birthday parties. Book online or call (914) 402-4624.

Are you skater-owned?

Yes. Built by Doug Brown + Jon DiCarlo — both lifelong skaters. The park runs on skateOS, software we're building to run skateparks better than any off-the-shelf POS can.
